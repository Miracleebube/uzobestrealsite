SAFETY POLICY

It is the policy of our company to use objective and tested methods to prevent accident in and out of our projects all the time, as we believe that all accident are prevented. It is the policy provision for Uzobest construction Limited to ensure that her entire employees, sub-contractors and all those involved in her operations are always healthy, secured and protected from hazards associated with the work and environment. For effective prevention of accidents, job safety analysis must be carried out as per each operation and be effectively communicated to the workers before and during the tool box meetings. Safety red signs, PPEs must be in place before commencement of any operation. Full accident/ incident investigations and review must be carried out and learning points made known to the entire workforce to avoid re-occurrence. SAFETY is of mutual interest and requires co-operation of the management and entire staff both of who are equally accountable for the attainment of this policy provisions. The management reserves the

right to revoke the services of any of her employees who is considered to be a violator to this policy provisions. Uzobest Construction Limited must ensure those competent people are put in place to a man areas that are critical and prone to accidents. Periodic Safety audits must be instituted to ensure compliance to safety rules and regulations. The safety Co- coordinator shall be accountable to the implementation of all safety policies of the company.

DRUG AND ALCOHOL POLICY

Uzobest Construction Ltd. Recognizes the danger posed by the abuse of drugs and alcohol hence it shall not tolerate the possession of any hard or banned drugs on any site, as such employee discovered to contravene this will be suspended from work. It is also prohibited for any employee to use, distribute or sell alcohol or illegal drugs on company business, work location and project vehicles. Uzobest Construction Ltd. Shall take disciplinary actions against any employee who due to illegal drugs or alcohol use in unable to work except where such person is undergoing medical help. We shall also conduct unannounced search for the drugs and alcohol at work location, office or in project vehicle. Personnel reported to have been under the influence of alcohol while driving shall be punished as may be considered by the CASH disciplinary committee.

1. Employees must adhere to the Journey Management Policy UZOBEST CONSTRUCTION LTD.

2. All authorized journey are PROHIBITED. All journeys Must be planned and on time too.

3. Project vehicle MUST be used within the assigned project areas.

4. Unauthorized passenger are NOT ALLOWED in the project vehicles.

5. Faulty vehicles should be reported to the manager for IMMEDIATE repairs.

6. OBEY all trafic and township safe parking rules.

7. All accident, near-miss incident and hazard MUST be reported correctly and promptly to HSE office.

8. Drivers/project personnel should have knowledge of the vehicle assigned to them.

9. Drivers/field worker shall ensure the safety and security of vehicle assigned to them.

10. In case of vehicle breaking-down on the road while traveling, the driver should arrange for security to guard the vehicle while he comes to the office to report or alternatively phone to the office.

11. Drivers must be required to have SPDC certificate driving permit in addition to valid Nigeria Driver's License.

12. Maximum speed limited of 100 km/hr. is authorized for the highways.

13. Maximum speed limit of 30 km/hr. for built up area is authorized.

14. No driving or sailing after 6.00 pm.

15. Project vehicle should be kept clean.

16. Diesel, double-powered engine boat shall be used on our SWAMP operations.

17. All vehicle must be equipped with intro Vehicle Monitoring System (IVMS) that must be downloaded monthly for analysis.

18. Management shall educate and review the performance of the Journey Management Policy on all staff.
